Biography

Meet Karrar Alsaadi, a talented singer and composer from Baghdad, now based in Vienna. His music beautifully blends jazz with Iraqi influences, reflecting his deep love for music since his upbringing in the vibrant soundscape of 1990s Iraq. Karrar made history as the first Iraqi artist to collaborate with renowned musicians like Radiohead's Jonny Greenwood and Dudu Tassa on the acclaimed album "Jarak Qaribak," which was nominated for Album of the Year 2023. His dedication to pushing musical boundaries is evident in every track. With a passion for bridging his Baghdadi roots with contemporary sounds, Karrar is a true musical innovator. His compositions breathe new life into traditional songs, honoring the past while embracing the future. Through his art, he preserves the essence of what conflict has torn apart, showcasing the transformative power of music.
